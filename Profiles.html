The "Traditionalist" Believer

Traditionalist are the second smallest group, accounting for 17% of all believers. These individuals are strongly aligned with institutional norms, scoring highest on Ecclesiastical Authority and Conventional Orthopraxy. They display clear resistance to progressive trends and individual interpretation, with moderately low scores on Social Progressiveness, Doctrinal Heterodoxy, and Self-Directed Religiosity. Their very low score on Pacifism signals the strongest endorsement that violence at times can be justified. This profile reflects deeply conservative and institutionally loyal members.
